The Hand-Cam

As humans we spend so much time with our normal perception that it seems normal to not even think about what life might be like if we saw the world from a different perspective. Well, I ain't one to abide by normalcy, so I sought to answer the one really important question facing our nation. What if our eyes were located somewhere else on our body, somewhere that's constantly in motion? Thus the hand-cam was born to document a day in my life from an odd angle.
