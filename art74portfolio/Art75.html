Art 75

Intro to Digital Video was a cool class for me. I never really thought I would be into video, but it's actually quite fun to work with. especially once you break into using After Effects and can throw all the rules of reality into a dumpster on the dark side of Mars. The class was broken down into four project each made up of a couple videos fousing on a single theme. The first project (videos 1-6) revolved around us just getting a grip on how to use our cameras, and how to use Adobe Premiere. The second (videos 7-10) where all about perspective and surveilance, while the third project (videos 11-13) is all about special effects. The Final project will be a presentation, but unfortunately it and project three will not be completed by the time this portfolio goes up.
